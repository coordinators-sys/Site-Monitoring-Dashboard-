Glossary

CCCM
Camp Coordination and Camp Management — the humanitarian cluster responsible for displacement sites.
IM
Information Management — the cluster function that validates and publishes these figures.
CA
Catchment Area — a group of nearby displacement sites managed as one operational unit.
HH
Household — the counting unit for population figures alongside individuals.
IDP
Internally Displaced Person.
SNFI / NFI
Shelter and Non-Food Items — non-food relief such as blankets, mats and kitchen sets.
WASH
Water, Sanitation and Hygiene.
FSL
Food Security and Livelihoods.
GBV
Gender-Based Violence.
HLP
Housing, Land and Property.
CMC
Camp Management Committee — the site-level representative body.
CFM
Complaint and Feedback Mechanism.
UNDP
United Nations Development Programme — source of the Admin-2 district boundaries.
OCHA
UN Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs — co-source of those boundaries.
Severity Score
Mean share of indicators scored Red across the sectors assessed at a site. Sectors not assessed are excluded, never counted as zero.
