National Overview

Q2 2026 covers sites and catchment areas where CCCM partners are actively present — it is not a census of all displacement sites. Each site is scored Green/Yellow/Red per indicator; the Severity Score is the average share of red-scored indicators across assessed sectors (higher = wider service gap).
