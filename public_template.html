Help & Glossary

This dashboard shows officially published CCCM Cluster Q2 2026 results only. Terms used throughout:

CA (Catchment Area)
A cluster of nearby IDP sites managed as one operational unit.
Severity Score
The share of assessed indicators scoring Red at a site, district or nationally.
Applicable base / denominator
The number of sites where a question could actually be answered — sites where it was Not Applicable are excluded.
Green / Yellow / Red
Green = standard met. Yellow = partially met. Red = gap or not met.
CFM
Complaint and Feedback Mechanism.
CMC
Camp Management Committee.
HLP
Housing, Land and Property.
GBV
Gender-Based Violence.
NFI
Non-Food Items.
